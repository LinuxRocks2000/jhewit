Welcome to the Avraham Society! If you are looking for the Jewish Humanist voice of the MENA region then you came to the right place! We are the home of future leaders who want to make a change in the world. We are an opportunity for all Jews and everything Jewish or Jew-ish! This is our oasis of Jewish Culture and a celebration of Humanism and Humanity!
